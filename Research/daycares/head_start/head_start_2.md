## Other Florida Head Start operators with explicit disability language (recap + context)

These were already mentioned but are part of the “all operators I can find with clear disability wording” picture:

- **4C (Community Coordinated Care for Children, Inc.) – Head Start / Early Head Start**
    - “Head Start is a federally funded program for low‑income children and **children with disabilities**…”[^4]
- **Episcopal Children’s Services – Head Start / Early Head Start**
    - “Early Head Start… serves… families of infants and toddlers, **including children with disabilities**. Head Start… serves preschool‑age children and **children with disabilities**.”[^5]
- **Lutheran Services Florida (LSF) Head Start** – FAQ notes that Head Start accepts “**children with disabilities or special needs**.”[^6]
- **You Thrive Florida Head Start** – “focusing on those experiencing homelessness or **with disabilities**.”[^3]
- **Hillsborough County Head Start/Early Head Start** – “including those with **special needs**.”[^2]
- **Easterseals Head Start Centers (North County)** – Head Start centers operated by Easterseals South Florida that serve “children and youth with **autism** from birth through age 22.”[^7]
- **Statewide / federal descriptions applied to all Florida grantees**
    - Florida Head Start Association: “center‑based programs… foster the development of children, **including children experiencing homelessness and disabilities**…”[^8]
    - HeadStart.gov “Children with Disabilities”: focuses on “children with disabilities and other **special needs**” across all Head Start programs.[^9]
    - UCF‑CARD Florida Head Start description: “Up to **10% of Head Start openings may be used by children with disabilities**…”[^10]

These additions (especially the full Hillsborough center list and You Thrive Florida Head Start) are the further Florida Head Start / Early Head Start operators and centers I can concretely tie to explicit disability/special‑needs language beyond the ones in the previous message.[^4][^5][^6][^7][^8][^9][^10][^3][^1]
