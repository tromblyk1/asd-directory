About TLC comfort care Industries

Our Mission

At 365 Neighbors Care, doing business as TLC Comfort Care Industries, our mission is to foster compassionate, year-round support for individuals, seniors, and families by building stronger, more connected communities. Since 2019, we have been dedicated to providing reliable, personalized care services—including non-emergency medical transportation (NEMT), independent living facility (ILF), companion care, and adult day training—across Central Florida. We prioritize safety, comfort, and dignity in everything we do, ensuring each individual receives the attention and respect they deserve. Our goal is to empower our clients, uplift our communities, and enhance quality of life with professionalism, integrity, and compassion.

About TLC Comfort Care Industries

365 Neighbors Care is a non profit organization doing business as TLC Comfort Care Industries. At TLC Comfort Care, we are dedicated to providing compassionate and reliable non-emergency medical transportation, non-medical home care, independent living facility & adult day training services throughout Central Florida. Since 2019, we have been serving individuals with unique needs, offering services with professionalism, integrity, and respect.

Our specialized transportation solutions cater to various needs, including bariatric transport, wheelchair assistance, ambulatory transport, and special trips, ensuring that our clients receive safe, comfortable, and efficient care. Whether it’s an NEMT transport, a routine medical appointment, or a special event, we are here to provide peace of mind and the highest level of care.

In addition to NEMT transportation, we offer in-home companion care, Independent living facility and adult day training services, ensuring that our clients have access to the support they need at home or in our training center located in Winter Haven, Florida. Every service we provide is designed with the goal of making our clients’ lives easier, safer, and more comfortable.

At TLC Comfort Care Industries, we understand that every individual is unique, and we strive to offer personalized solutions to meet their needs. Our team is highly trained and dedicated to delivering exceptional care, providing support for clients and families with kindness and professionalism.

We are committed to improving the lives of those we serve, and we do so with the utmost respect, compassion, and attention to detail.

The adult day training center located in Winter Haven, Florida. TLC Comfort Care Industries, offers specialized services designed to support individuals with developmental disabilities, physical impairments, and other needs. It provides a safe, welcoming, and structured environment where clients can engage in meaningful activities to enhance their skills, independence, and social interaction.

The center focuses on creating personalized plans for each participant, offering a range of activities such as life skills training, vocational development, social engagement, physical exercises, and recreational activities. The goal is to improve quality of life while promoting self-esteem and personal growth.

The facility is equipped with resources to meet the needs of the clients, ensuring that they receive appropriate care and attention. Staff members are trained to provide compassionate and professional assistance, creating an environment where individuals feel respected and valued.
